Implementation time varies depending on the complexity and scope of the service. For example, launching a Service Desk can take 4 to 6 weeks, while a complete financial outsourcing could require 2 to 4 months. In all cases, we use phased planning to ensure a smooth and seamless transition.

Service quality is guaranteed by a signed Service Level Agreement (SLA) detailing key metrics (such as response time, availability, and accuracy). We also ensure compliance through periodic audits and detailed performance reports.

We designate a single Account Manager as your primary contact to ensure smooth coordination and consistent understanding.

Security and confidentiality are our top priority. We reinforce this commitment by signing strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs).
