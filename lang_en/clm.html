Contract Life Cycle Management (CLM)

Bigle Official Partner

Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) is a software tool that enables companies to automate, secure and control the document lifecycle from start to finish, reducing management times and mitigating risks.


Functionalities


Document Review and Summarization

Analyzes and synthesizes documents, making them easier to understand and allowing for time optimization.

AI-Powered Queries

Answer questions and resolve doubts about a document using AI, streamlining the search for relevant information and the analysis of legal contingencies.

Quality Legal Drafting

Drafts high-quality and consistent legal content, including all types of clauses and contracts, tailored to the analyzed document.

One-Click Translation

Translates complex legal documents into 20 languages with unprecedented accuracy.

Benefits

- Control and traceability of document operations
- Reduction of legal risks
- Improves internal control
- Document automation
- Facilitates collaboration between areas and democratizes its use by generating a self-service document system for the entire company

Features

- Single Sign-On (SSO) via SAML
- ISO 27001 Certification
- Data encryption in transit and at rest for all
- Secure servers in the EU provided by a Tier 1 CSP
