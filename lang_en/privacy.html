Cookies

A cookie is a file that is sent for the purpose of requesting permission to be stored on your computer. By accepting this file, the cookie is created and then serves to obtain information regarding web traffic, and also facilitates future visits to a recurring website. Our website uses cookies to identify the pages that are visited and their frequency. This information is used only for statistical analysis and then the information is permanently deleted. The user can delete cookies at any time from their computer. However, cookies help to provide a better service to websites. They do not give access to information on your computer or about you, unless you want it and provide it directly. The user can accept or deny the use of cookies, however most browsers accept cookies automatically as it serves to have a better web service. The user can also change the configuration of their computer to decline cookies. If they are declined, it is possible that you will not be able to use some of our services.
